Column descriptions

APOGEE_ID: identification number

REGION: Star-forming region or stellar population to which the star belongs

abun_Fe_log: Logarithmic iron abundance relative to hydrogen, [Fe/H]
MAD_Fe_log: Median Absolute Deviation (MAD) of the logarithmic iron abundance, used as an estimate of the abundance uncertainty
abun_Fe_sol: Iron abundance relative to the solar value, [Fe/H]⊙
MAD_Fe_sol: Median Absolute Deviation (MAD) of the iron abundance relative to the solar value

MAD_Al_sol: Median Absolute Deviation (MAD) of the titanium abundance relative to the solar value
