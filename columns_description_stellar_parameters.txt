Column descriptions

OBJ     : 2MASS source identification number
RA      : Right Ascension (J2000)
DEC     : Declination (J2000)
REGION  : Name of the star-forming region

t25     : Effective temperature at the 25th percentile
t50     : Effective temperature at the 50th percentile (median)
t75     : Effective temperature at the 75th percentile

g25     : Surface gravity at the 25th percentile
g50     : Surface gravity at the 50th percentile (median)
g75     : Surface gravity at the 75th percentile

m25     : Global metallicity at the 25th percentile
m50     : Global metallicity at the 50th percentile (median)
m75     : Global metallicity at the 75th percentile

a25     : Alpha-element abundance at the 25th percentile
a50     : Alpha-element abundance at the 50th percentile (median)
a75     : Alpha-element abundance at the 75th percentile

v25     : Projected rotational velocity (v sin i) at the 25th percentile
v50     : Projected rotational velocity (v sin i) at the 50th percentile (median)
v75     : Projected rotational velocity (v sin i) at the 75th percentile
